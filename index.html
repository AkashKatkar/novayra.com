About Novayra - Luxury Perfume Brand

Novayra was born in Mumbai from a passion for creating luxury fragrances that transcend the ordinary. Our master perfumers blend the world's finest ingredients, including rare Indian essences, to craft modern perfumes that tell stories, evoke emotions, and create lasting memories with their unique scent profiles.

Each Novayra fragrance is a testament to luxury, crafted with precision and care to deliver a radiant essence that captivates the senses. Drawing inspiration from Mumbai's cosmopolitan culture and India's rich heritage of perfumery, we believe that scent is the most powerful sense, capable of transporting us to different times and places, awakening memories, and creating new ones with every spritz of our premium perfumes.
